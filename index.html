How to Use CSV PlotStudio

Step 1: Upload your CSV file using the upload area above

Step 2: Review the data preview to understand your dataset structure

Step 3: Select which columns to plot on X and Y axes

Step 4: Choose your preferred chart type (Line, Scatter, or Bar)

Step 5: Click "Generate Chart" to create your visualization


Note: Y-axis must be numeric data for proper plotting
